Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your Swift FX account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to: support@swiftfxsignals.com

Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request

Include the email address associated with your Swift FX account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile information
  • Authentication records
  • Subscription status records
  • Forum posts and comments
  • Associated usage data

Data Retention Policy

Upon verification of your request, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.

Some transaction records may be retained where required for legal, tax, or financial compliance purposes.

Contact

If you have questions regarding data deletion, contact:

support@swiftfxsignals.com
